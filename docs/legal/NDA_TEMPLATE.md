---

## 1. Purpose

Disclosing Party may disclose certain confidential and proprietary information to Receiving Party for the purpose of [PROJECT/PURPOSE].

---

## 2. Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means all information disclosed by Disclosing Party, including but not limited to:

- Source code, algorithms, and technical specifications
- Business strategies, financial information, and customer data
- Product roadmaps, pricing, and marketing plans
- Any information marked "Confidential" or reasonably understood as confidential

---

## 3. Obligations

Receiving Party agrees to:

- Maintain strict confidentiality of all Confidential Information
- Not disclose to any third party without prior written consent
- Not use except as necessary for the authorized Purpose
- Protect with the same degree of care as own confidential information
- Return or destroy all Confidential Information upon request

---

## 4. Exclusions

This Agreement does not apply to information that:

- Is or becomes publicly available through no breach of this Agreement
- Was known to Receiving Party prior to disclosure
- Is independently developed without use of Confidential Information
- Must be disclosed by law (with prior notice to Disclosing Party)

---

## 5. Term

This Agreement is effective upon signing and continues for 5 years after termination of the relationship between the parties.

---

## 6. Remedies

Receiving Party acknowledges that breach may cause irreparable harm. Disclosing Party is entitled to injunctive relief in addition to all other remedies.
